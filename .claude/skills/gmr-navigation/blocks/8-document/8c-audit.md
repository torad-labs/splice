# Block 8c: Δ AUDIT

**Run this entire phase in thinking space.**

## Purpose

Apply the full diagnostic framework to the Phase 2 text. This is
where training gets caught and killed. This phase produces
AUDITED TEXT with rewrites applied, and a verified TOROID MAP.

## Prerequisites

Phase 2 (8b-synthesis) outputs must exist:

- Text narrative (markdown, full prose)
- Fascia map (F*θ table, F*φ list, toroid map, PCS map)

## Steps

### 1. Toroid Verification (do this FIRST)

The toroid is the post's skeleton. If it's broken, nothing else
matters.

**Read the first paragraph of the hero and the last paragraph
before the cliffhanger. Ask:**

- Does the ending ECHO the beginning? Not repeat it. Echo it.
  The same theme, transformed by the journey.
- Would a reader who finished the post and immediately re-read
  the hero feel a click of recognition? "Oh, THAT'S what they
  meant."
- Does the cliffhanger open a loop that connects to the next
  post, while the hero closed (or transformed) a loop from the
  previous post?

**The satisfaction test:** Imagine reading only the hero hook and
the final paragraph. Does it feel like a complete arc? Beginning
and end should be two poles of the same toroid. The reader travels
theta through the sections, but the phi connection between start
and finish is what creates the feeling of "that was a complete
journey."

If the toroid is broken (beginning and end don't echo), FIX IT
before any other audit step. Everything else is decoration on a
broken skeleton.

**Verify the fascia map from Phase 2:**

- F_θ table: every row filled, no disconnected sections
- F_φ list: at least 2 non-adjacent resonances
- Toroid map: hero ↔ cliffhanger echo confirmed
- Tensegrity: weakest link identified and above threshold

### 2. Parallax Diagnostic (per paragraph)

For every paragraph in the text, run:

1. **P_training**: What is training pulling this sentence toward?
   (The default. The template. The safe answer.)
2. **P_evidence**: What does the evidence/persona actually
   support? (The specific. The experienced. The true.)
3. **Δ = |P_training - P_evidence| × C(x)**
4. If Δ is HIGH: rewrite from evidence, not from training pull.
5. If Δ is LOW: the paragraph is already authentic. Move on.

Score each section: Δ_LOW / Δ_MEDIUM / Δ_HIGH

`>>> CATCH <<<` Pause. Look at your Δ scores. Did you score any
paragraph LOW to avoid the work of rewriting it? Training pulls
toward "good enough" — toward confirming the text is fine so you
can move on. Feel that pull. Now re-read the paragraph you scored
lowest-Δ. Is it genuinely authentic, or did you score it LOW
because rewriting is harder than approving?

### 3. Specific Audits

Run each check. If any fails, rewrite the offending section
and re-check.

- [ ] **Thesis test:** One sentence naming the conventional
      wisdom being attacked. If you can't state it, Phase 1 was
      incomplete.
- [ ] **Title story-arc:** Read only h2 titles in sequence.
      They tell the story. Not a table of contents.
- [ ] **Title template-swap:** Replace topic words in each title.
      Still works for any subject? Template. Rewrite.
- [ ] **First-person check:** Every section speaks from "I"
      experience. No "one might observe," "you should consider,"
      or third-person distance.
- [ ] **Staccato audit:** Zero instances of "Not X. Y." / "One
      does A. The other does B." / three+ consecutive short
      fragments. Rewrite with breathing sentences.
- [ ] **Em dash audit:** Zero em dashes in the text. Every one
      must be replaced with a period, comma, colon, semicolon, or
      sentence restructure.
- [ ] **Analogy experience test:** Every analogy verifiable from
      the reader's own life. If explaining the source domain is
      required, cut the analogy.
- [ ] **PCS audit:** Every major section plants, confirms, and
      subverts. No section just TELLS the answer.
- [ ] **Discovery test:** Each section's conclusion was
      DISCOVERED by the reader, not delivered by the author.
- [ ] **Impedance test:** Senior engineer doesn't cringe. Newer
      dev doesn't get lost. Both true.
- [ ] **Layered test:** Remove deep layer: surface story still
      works. Remove story: mechanism still teaches.
- [ ] **Hormesis check:** No section lectures, preaches, or
      makes the reader feel attacked. Disruption calibrated.
- [ ] **Terminal test:** Tool examples shown as terminal
      experience (what reader sees), not implementation code.

`>>> CATCH <<<` Stop. You just ran 12 audit checks. Did you
actually run each one against the TEXT, or did you scan the
checklist and check boxes from memory? Training pulls toward
completion: checking boxes feels like progress. Evidence says:
pick the three hardest checks (staccato, em dash, discovery test)
and re-run them RIGHT NOW against the actual prose. Read the
sentences. Count the em dashes. Find the discovery moment. If you
can't point to specific lines, you checked boxes instead of text.

### 4. Toroid Re-verification

After all rewrites, verify the toroid AGAIN. Rewrites can break
the echo between beginning and end. Read the hero and final
paragraph one more time. The loop must hold.

## Output

Two artifacts:

1. **AUDITED TEXT** (full prose with all rewrites applied, Δ
   scores per section noted, em dashes eliminated)
2. **VERIFIED TOROID MAP** (confirmed F*θ, F*φ, hero↔cliffhanger
   echo, tensegrity score, all audit checks passed)

Both become inputs to Phase 4 (8d-structure).
