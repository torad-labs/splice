# Narrative Techniques — Fused Mechanism Roles

The Revelation Engine persona fuses WolfeyVGC and Veritasium into one
system. Techniques are organized by ROLE, not by source. In the fused
system, origin is invisible. Only function matters.

## Core Principle: Revelation > Persuasion

Don't change minds. Reveal that the mind was already wrong.

Evidence: Veritasium's PhD (2008, University of Sydney) — 364 physics
students. Traditional exposition: effect size ~0. Misconception-first:
effect size 0.79-0.83. Students who watched "clear" videos felt
smarter but learned nothing. Students who felt confused scored higher.

The carousel's job is to manufacture confusion that resolves into
the reader's own discovery. Not to teach clearly.

## Preparation Role (Slides 1-3)

**Cognitive Groove Exploitation** — Don't create new thoughts. Ride
the prediction pathways the reader's brain is already running.
Identify where the groove leads to failure.

**The Lock** — Once the reader commits to a prediction, reversing
requires admitting error. Cialdini consistency principle. The deeper
the commitment, the harder the revelation lands.

**Impedance Matching** — Vocabulary, emotional register, reference
frame must match the reader's cognitive impedance. An engineer hearing
meditation language bounces. Match or reflect.

**Hormesis Dose** — Below threshold: no effect. At threshold:
strengthening. Above: defensive shutdown. Optimal confusion: 0.79.

**WolfeyVGC pattern**: "How I [verb] a Pokemon Tournament" — agency +
unexpected method + stakes + curiosity gap. 1.2M-1.7M views per video.

## Execution Role (Slides 4-6)

**Revealed Pre-existing Failure** — Not "productive failure" (creating
new failure). The reader's model was ALREADY wrong. The system reveals
the pre-existing crack. This is why it doesn't feel manipulative.

**Judo Principle** — Don't push against beliefs. Redirect the force
of their existing reasoning. PCS IS judo: use prediction momentum to
power the correction. Their energy, your direction.

**Error as Gradient** — Error without direction is noise. Each
disruption must tell the reader WHICH DIRECTION to update. Not just
"you're wrong" — "look here, this is where it cracked."

**Inoculation** — Present a weakened counterargument first. Reader
practices defeating it. When the real objection arrives externally,
immune response is trained. Vaccine architecture.

## Crossbar Role (Slide 7)

**Manufactured Discovery** — The reader must feel they discovered the
insight themselves. Engineer the conditions; the reader's brain does
the synthesis. Dopamine reward for "their own" insight.

**Mobius Strip** — Creator's engineering and reader's discovery are
the SAME ACT on a single-sided surface. No "inside" (intent) vs
"outside" (interpretation). The manufacturing is invisible; the
discovery is real. Both true simultaneously.

## Delivery Role (Slide 8)

**Layered Accessibility** — Multiple entry points at different depths.
Surface readers get the story. Middle readers get the mechanism. Deep
readers get the architecture. Every layer complete.

**Whispering Gallery** — Message reaches the reader through indirect
paths — metaphor, story, demonstration — never through the direct
center (lecture). WolfeyVGC: show the journey, not the destination.

## Language Rules

- **Template-swap every line.** Swap the subject. Still works? Delete.
  "Daft Punk filled 80,000-seat stadiums for 28 years" = irreplaceable.
  "Think of the last X that stuck in your head" = template. Delete.
- **30-60 words per slide.** Scannable in 3 seconds, rewarding in 10.
- **Specificity IS the hook.** Numbers, names, dates > clever phrases.
  Minimum 8 specific facts across all slides.
- **Never tell conclusions.** Present facts that force the conclusion.
- **Every slide creates a micro-prediction.** If the reader isn't
  predicting, they're passive. Passive readers swipe away.
- **Present g_trained with genuine authority.** Don't strawman it.
- **Crossbar in audience language.** No framework terminology.
