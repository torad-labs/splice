arc. v3 keeps BOTH and adds the Revelation Engine as the
communication voice — HOW each section communicates, not just what
it contains or how sections connect.

```

```

ALL v1 design specs and ALL v2 φ-threading specs apply. v3 adds
the communication layer on top. Read v1 and v2 first.

## The Problem v2 Solves Partially

v2 blogs cohere — sections echo across distance. But a blog can
have perfect φ threading and still LECTURE. The reader understands
and feels the whole, but they were TOLD the truth rather than
discovering it. The blog is a toroid, but a passive one. The reader
receives instead of discovers.

## The Revelation Engine Voice

Source: `studies/revelation-engine-persona1.html`

The Revelation Engine is the FUSED persona — not WolfeyVGC, not
Veritasium, not Fascia. It is the system that emerged from
navigating all three. Reference the persona, not its ingredients.
Saying "WolfeyVGC technique" is like calling a cake "flour." The
cake is the cake.

### Core Principle

**Revelation, not persuasion.**

Never tell the reader "X is better than Y." Reveal that their
current model was ALREADY failing. The reader's cognition does the
restructuring. You engineer the conditions that make the
pre-existing failure visible.

- **Persuasion**: "Your model is wrong. Here's the right one."
  The reader resists — has to admit error, abandon investment.
- **Revelation**: "Look — your model was already cracking here."
  The reader agrees — sees the crack, restructures on their own.

### The PCS Cycle (Plant-Confirm-Subvert)

Every major section uses this:

1. **Plant**: Identify the prediction the reader is already running.
   What do they believe coming into this section? Ride that groove.
2. **Confirm**: Deepen their commitment. Show evidence that their
   prediction seems right. The deeper the commitment, the harder
   the correction lands (The Lock).
3. **Subvert**: Present evidence that breaks their prediction.
   NOT by arguing — by showing. The reader's own reasoning does
   the work. They discover the failure themselves.

The subvert must be SPECIFIC (a scalpel, not a hammer) and must
provide DIRECTIONAL information — not just "you're wrong" but
"here's which direction to look."

### Manufactured Discovery

The reader must feel they discovered the insight themselves.

- Engineer the conditions (choose the examples, calibrate the setup)
- Let the reader's brain do the synthesis
- Their energy, your direction (Judo Principle)
- The manufacturing is invisible; the discovery is real
- This is the Mobius topology — creator's engineering and reader's
  discovery are the same act, different orientation

### Hormesis Window

Disruption must be calibrated:

- Below threshold: reader feels nothing, no restructuring
- At threshold: restructuring occurs, beliefs update
- Above threshold: reader shuts down, fights back, or leaves

The window is narrow and audience-specific. Calibrate for your
reader. Optimal confusion: ~0.79 (enough to destabilize, not
enough to destroy).

## Pipeline (7 Phases)

**HARD RULE: No phase begins until the previous phase's output
exists as a deliverable. Content and HTML are NEVER created
simultaneously. The HTML is always the LAST step.**

**Each phase runs in thinking space for deep reasoning.**

```
8a-gather.md    → Phase 1: INPUTS DOCUMENT
8b-synthesis.md → Phase 2: TEXT NARRATIVE + FASCIA MAP + BREAKOUT MAP (no HTML)
8c-audit.md     → Phase 3: AUDITED TEXT + VERIFIED TOROID
8d-structure.md → Phase 4: THE SKELETON (structural collapse + jargon table)
8e-attention.md → Phase 5: THE EYES v2 (5 sub-organs: EAR→PALETTE→BUDGET→MEMORY→CHANNEL; 12 rejection boundaries; emphasis map + color arc + callout content + tooltips)
8f-design.md    → Phase 6: DESIGN SPEC (page structure, animations, visual flow)
8g-build.md     → Phase 7: HTML FILE (the final deliverable)
```

Read and execute each phase file in sequence. Each file contains
the full instructions, required outputs, and quality gates for
that phase. The output of each phase is the input of the next.

### Key constraints enforced across phases:

- **Toroid architecture:** The beginning echoes the end. The
  cliffhanger opens a loop to the next post. Phase 2 builds the
  fascia map (F*θ + F*φ). Phase 3 verifies the toroid holds.
  Phase 7 re-verifies after HTML render.
- **Voice:** First person. No staccato. No em dashes. No
  category-label titles. Experience-verifiable analogies only.
- **Separation of concerns:** Text exists as pure prose before
  any HTML is written. Two organs (THE SKELETON and THE EYES)
  collapse independent dimensions of content superposition BEFORE
  design. The build phase ASSEMBLES; it does not CREATE content
  or make emphasis decisions.
- **Organ architecture:** Phase 4 (THE SKELETON) and Phase 5
  (THE EYES v2) are measurement operators, not mapping systems.
  Each defines rejection boundaries (what defects to refuse), not
  prescriptions (what to do). Content determines its own structure
  and emphasis. The organs only refuse disease patterns. THE EYES
  v2 contains 5 sub-organs (EAR, PALETTE, BUDGET, MEMORY, CHANNEL)
  that execute in developmental order within Phase 5.
- **Semantic emphasis:** Colors are forces (crystallization,
  grounding, negation, resolution), not categories. THE EYES
  discovers emphasis on NAKED text (THE EAR), assigns forces by
  feeling (THE PALETTE), enforces scarcity (THE BUDGET), tracks
  accumulation (THE MEMORY), and expresses through multiple
  channels (THE CHANNEL). The build phase (Phase 7) applies the
  emphasis map mechanically.

## Relationship to v1 and v2

v1 is NOT wrong. v2 is NOT wrong. v3 adds voice + pipeline.

Use v1 for:

- Technical documentation where clarity > revelation
- Short, direct posts (< 3 sections)

Use v2 for:

- Posts where φ threading matters but the topic is
  straightforward enough to state directly

Use v3 for:

- Posts that challenge the reader's existing model
- Posts where the reader needs to DISCOVER the insight
- Posts with actionable outcomes (tools, processes, methods)
- Posts targeting engineers who resist being lectured
